Lemon Bread

Description

Sweet not to sour but tasty dessert for any occasion. Easily made with plenty of differnt options to make it better. This simple recipe can be made in just a short time. This moist lemon tasting bread can easily be taken as a snack for lunch or eaten as a dessert after a good dinner.

Not only are you able to just enjoy this recipe by it self, you can spice things up with toppings! Personally my favorite topping is a little strawberry to sweeten it up a little more. Lots of other people out there have come up with some tasty options! Lets dive into how to make this recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 tablespoon grated lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup 2% milk
Glaze
  • 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°.
  2. In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy, 5-7 minutes.
  3. Beat in eggs, lemon zest and juice.
  4. Combine flour, baking powder and salt; gradually stir into creamed mixture alternately with milk, beating well after each addition.
  5. Pour into a greased 8x4-in. loaf pan.
  6. Bake until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, 40-45 minutes.
  7. Cool in pan 15 minutes before removing to a wire rack.
  8. Combine glaze ingredients; immediately drizzle over bread.
