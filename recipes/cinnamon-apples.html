Cinnamon Apples

Description

This recipe turns those normal apples into a tasty sweet dessert or even a snack! These cinnamon apples are perfect for that thanksgiving desert or for a nice fall day! With only a few ingredients and a little work, you can have this tasty treat ready to eat in just a short moment.

Just the cinnamon apples them self are super tasty and leaves you wanting more. But with a little imagination you can put some tasty topping on them to make them even more delicious! Some of my favorites are whipped cream, smashed strawberries, or even a little honey! There have been lots of more options people have thought about so don't be afraid to try some out that you think would be good!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon light butter
  • 1 tablespoon + 1/3 cup of water
  • 4 medium to large apples
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 3/4 teaspoon cinnamon

Steps

  1. Place the butter and a tablespoon of the water into a saut√© pan and bring over medium heat until butter is melted.
  2. Add the sliced apples and stir to coat with the butter.
  3. Cook uncovered, stirring occasionally for 4-5 minutes to soften the apples.
  4. In a small bowl, stir together the cornstarch and water until combined.
  5. Add this mixture to the apples along with the brown sugar and cinnamon.
  6. Stir until well combined.
  7. Cover the pan and reduce the heat to low.
  8. Simmer for 3-4 minutes until the apples are tender and the sauce is thickened.
