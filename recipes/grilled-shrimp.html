Marinated Grilled Shrimp

Description

Fire up the grill it's time to make a tasty home cooked shrimp dinner! Shrimp alone is good by it's self and we all know that but to make an even better dinner this marinated grilled shrimp dinner will have your family or even guest ask you what the recipe is! With a short cook time you can have dinner ready in just a couple minutes!

This shirmp recipe is marinated in some amazing spices to have a mouth full of flavor. Along side those spices you get a nice evenly cooked shrimp. Throwing a little pineapple in with this recipe and you can a nice sweet taste along with the shrimp. It doesn't end there Let your imagination run wild and you can create all sorts of dishes with this recipe! Lets get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 pounds fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 6 each skewers

Steps

  1. Stir together olive oil, tomato sauce, garlic, and red wine vinegar in a large bowl. Season with basil, salt, and cayenne pepper. Add shrimp to the bowl; stir until evenly coated. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes to 1 hour, stirring once or twice.
  2. Preheat the grill to medium heat. Lightly oil the grate.
  3. Thread shrimp onto skewers, piercing once near the tail and once near the head. Discard marinade.
  4. Cook shrimp on the preheated grill until opaque, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
